About Cortex Club

Cortex Club is Oxford University's leading neuroscience society, dedicated to fostering scientific curiosity and advancing our understanding of the nervous system.

We bring together students, researchers, and faculty across various disciplines to explore the complexities of the brain. Through table talks, interactive workshops, and collaborative research projects, we create a vibrant community passionate about neuroscience.
